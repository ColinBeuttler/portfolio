Developer Bio

My name is Colin Beuttler, I am a recent graduate of UCI's coding boot camp and I have created this site in order to show off a few of my past projects. The sites featured are projects I worked on with others in order to sharpen my skills as a Developer. In the past I have used many technologies such as jQuery, React, and Parallax. I am constantly looking into new technologies and enjoy learning about their strengths and trade offs. If you have any questions for me or want to know more about any of my experiences or the technologies on this site I have included my email as well as a contact form at the bottom of this page.

Technologies Used on This Site

Pure CSS

Pure CSS is a lightweight and responsive framework that features a very clean and minimalistic aesthetic. This website was my first exposure to Pure, I used in hopes it would bring subtle style changes to my site.

jQuery

jQuery is a lightweight and fast library used with JavaScript. I've used jQuery in the past and found it to be very helpful. I especially like using it in conjunction with parallax.

Previous Works

Weather Blog

This Blog was designed for users to post current weather conditions within their area. I mostly worked on the blog form, connecting it to the backend and the UI. My favorite part of this site was our use of parallax. It was my first time I had ever used it and was the main reason I included it into my portfolio.

Go Play Ruff

This app was designed to help users determine which restaurants around them they could dine in with their dogs. This was my first introduction to react. I was tasked with the tile puzzle and the backend of the login. I had not made any games with JavaScript, let alone React, but I was really pleased with the end. I hope to include other puzzle games such as this one in many future projects.
